Setting the Mood: Dining on the Drive

Wilton Manors isn't just about party vibes. It hides some of South Florida's most charming culinary gems. Whether you're celebrating an anniversary or enjoying a first date, these spots offer the perfect lighting, noise level, and menu for romance.

Our Top 4 Picks

1. Le Patio

The Vibe: Intimate, quiet, and undeniably charming. Often called the "tiniest, cutest restaurant" in the area, it feels like a secret garden.

Why it’s Romantic: It’s adults-only and focused on conversation. The noise level is low, allowing you to actually hear your date. Perfect for a cozy European-style dinner.

2. Union Kitchen & Bar

The Vibe: Modern, warm, and energetic without being loud. The decor is impressive, featuring beautiful murals and a lovely patio.

The Food: Known for their made-from-scratch pasta and fresh, seasonal menu. Everything feels crafted with care—ideal for sharing dishes.

3. Ethos Greek Bistro

The Vibe: Upscale Mediterranean with a fantastic outdoor seating area. It sits right on the Drive, offering great people-watching while maintaining an elegant dining experience.

The Food: Fresh seafood, lamb chops, and dips. It’s a great mix of casual elegance.

4. Casa D'Angelo (Fort Lauderdale)

The Vibe: A short trip just outside Wilton Manors, this is the classic "Special Occasion" Italian spot. White tablecloths, business-casual dress code, and impeccable service.

The Food: World-class Italian. If you really want to splurge and impress, this is the reservation to make (call in advance!).

Don't want the night to end? Skip the loud clubs and head to Infinity Lounge or DrYnk for a sophisticated nightcap where you can continue your conversation over a craft cocktail.

Dining FAQ

Do I need reservations?
For places like Casa D'Angelo and Union Kitchen on weekends, yes, we highly recommend booking a few days in advance.
Is there a dress code?
Wilton Manors is generally "resort casual," but Casa D'Angelo prefers a smarter look (collared shirts, no beachwear).
