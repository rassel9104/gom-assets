Beyond the Party: The Daytime Side of Wilton Manors

Wilton Manors is legendary for its neon lights and nightlife, but when the sun comes up, the "Island City" reveals a relaxed, cultural, and adventurous side that defines the true local lifestyle.

Day & Night

Discover tropical charm under the sun.

Eco-Adventure in the Island City

Surrounded by the forks of the Middle River, Wilton Manors is an unsuspecting paradise for water lovers. Renting a kayak or paddleboard is one of the best ways to see the local architecture and wildlife (herons, iguanas, and manatees) from a different perspective.

The Brunch Culture

In our community, brunch isn't just a meal, it's a ritual. From bottomless mimosas to gourmet eggs benedict, Sundays (and Saturdays) are reserved for recovery and reconnection.

"The rhythm of Wilton Manors changes completely by day. It's the perfect time to stroll, shop at local boutiques, and enjoy an iced coffee without the rush."

Art & Culture

Galleries and Strolls

On the third Saturday of every month, the Art Walk transforms the Drive into an open-air gallery. But even on a normal day, you can find hidden treasures in antique shops and design boutiques that are unique to our area.

After a day of exploring, returning to the tranquility of Garden of Manors is the perfect closure. A dip in the pool before getting ready for the night is the quintessential routine of the honorary Wilton Manors resident.
