Discover Garden of Manors, an exclusive men-only boutique guesthouse in the heart of Wilton Manors, Fort Lauderdale. Our art and design-inspired retreat is an adult-only (21+) and clothing-optional sanctuary of calm, created for those seeking ultimate comfort, privacy, and the freedom to be themselves.

Choose between our private suites, the "Red Room" or the "Green Room," each designed for your relaxation. Unwind by our heated saltwater pool surrounded by lush tropical gardens, or enjoy the serenity on our intimate sundecks and outdoor lounges.

Located just steps from Wilton Drive, you’ll have the best LGBTQ+ atmosphere, dining, and nightlife in Fort Lauderdale right at your fingertips. Experience a true design oasis at the premier gay guesthouse in Wilton Manors.
